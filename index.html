Toorism

Every year, over 1.4 billion people travel abroad – more than ever before in human history. And tourism is often seen as a source of cultural exchange, economic opportunity, relaxation.
However, in recent years the new phenomenon of "overtourism" has appeared, symbolised by overcrowded streets and the proliferation of AirBnBs.
More generally, we have started to ask ourselves: what if tourism meant more than just sunny beaches and holidays? What effects does it have on the locals? And most importantly, who gets to be a tourist and who doesn't?

The Travel and Tourism Development Index (TTDI) measures how well a country enables sustainable and resilient travel and tourism through factors like infrastructure, environment, and policy.
